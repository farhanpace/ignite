Enrich your passion. Match your interests.

Ignite the Sound is the first gathering of audio content creators in Saudi Arabia, connecting creators and interested parties with local and international experts in the field of sound. This inaugural event aims to promote opportunities, enrich the local market and enhance the creative potential of sound creators .

Ignite the Sound will pave the way for new opportunities in the field of sound and podcasting, in an unparalleled gathering that will provide a platform for hobbyist, professionals and brands to share knowledge and exchange interest. The program will empower talent from around the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to reach their full potential and share their work around the world in a professional, meaningful and successful way. The in-person event will offer unique experiences for visitors with stories to be told and voices to be shared.
